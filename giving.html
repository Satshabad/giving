Guru Ram Das Giving Fund

Philanthropic Mission Statement

Our mission

Create future equality by empowering black girls today.

Guru Ram Das was the 4th teacher of Sikhism, a religion that originates in India. Guru Ram Das embodied service, and humility. He established the Golden Temple in Amritsar, India which feeds 100,000 people per day regardless of religion, race, or gender. Everyone sits and eats together on the same level, royalty and impoverished alike, symbolizing the equal humanity of every person.

Our mission is that everyone may recognize the equal humanity in each other. When people recognize the other person as themselves, discrimination and violence will be replaced with service and humility.

The biggest barriers to an equal future for all people are racial and gender inequalities. Any future with pervasive sexism and racism will be unjust and prevent all people from truly recognizing the equal humanity in each other.

We believe by empowering young black girls now we can have the greatest impact on future equality for all.
